FAQ

How do I play Catch the Offer?
Move your basket to catch falling balls and rewards. Be careful — if you catch a bomb, your game will end instantly! Offers and balls drop separately — so keep your eyes sharp!
What are the game rules?
  • Use the basket to catch falling balls and rewards.
  • If a bomb is caught, the game ends immediately.
  • Each missed ball reduces 1 life.
  • You have a maximum of 3 lives.
Can I restore lives?
Yes! If you catch 5 balls in a row without missing, you’ll regain 1 lost life (only if you're below 3). However, if even 1 ball is missed during those 5, no life will be restored.
How does offer redemption work?
You can redeem only 1 offer every 24 hours. Once redeemed, your cart will reset and you must play again to earn new offers.
What happens when the cart resets?
After 24 hours, your cart will clear automatically. To collect new rewards, play again and catch more offers in the game.
